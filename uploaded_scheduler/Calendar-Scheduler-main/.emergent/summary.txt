**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Main Page:** A calendar view of events. Default should be a week view. A smaller monthly calendar on the side should allow for navigation. A full-screen monthly calendar view should also be available for hosts.
- **Event Claiming:** Employees can claim an event (one employee per event) with a confirmation pop-up. They can also un-assign themselves.
- **Host Login:** Hosts must log in to view the schedule. The primary login method should be Google OAuth. A secondary option using username/password (name from dropdown, password B1GHat) should be available as a fallback.

- **Payment & Reporting:**
    - **Weekly Report:** A report for admins to see who worked what, where, and the event type for payroll, with a payment acknowledgment feature.
    - **Monthly Report:**
        - A tab for admins to track incoming venue payments against outgoing host payments.
        - It should feature a full-size calendar to visualize the schedule.
        - Admins should be able to quickly schedule new events by clicking on dates in this calendar.
        - The calendar should also display major external events (concerts, sports) happening nearby to avoid conflicts.
